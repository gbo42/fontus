About Fontus

A project born from the urge to save one of the most important and scarce elements on our planet: drinking water.

Made by two students from the south of Brazil, intends to help people visualize the water consumption on their houses, condos, companies and even industries.

Along the way we built a web app that permits user to access data through tables and graphs that works with a kit of hardware to be installed in liquid containers.
